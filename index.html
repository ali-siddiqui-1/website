How will you run promotions without damaging the relationship with my list, hurting my brand, or annoying my audience?

The copy will be relationship-centric, with the goal being to build trust, authority, and "deposits in the emotional bank account" of your audience. Each promo piece will be crafted with the goal of generating sales whilst also connecting deeper with your audience.

There must be a balance - too much emphasis on the relationship tanks sales, and too much emphasis on getting the sale damages the relationship. It's a delicate equilibrium that our copywriters have been trained to strike. Rest assured, everything we create will be on-brand, in your voice and tonality.

We will also send the completed promo materials to you each month for your thoughts and feedback, helping us making smaller course-corrections if needed to ensure you feel aligned with everything before we get it live for you.
